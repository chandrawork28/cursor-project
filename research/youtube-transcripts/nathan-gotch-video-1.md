# Nathan Gotch Video Research

Video Title:
7-Step SEO Campaign Checklist (for 2026)

Video URL:
https://youtu.be/H_l6nQjrc0Y

Key Insights:

1. Successful SEO campaigns require a documented process rather than random optimization activities.

2. Keyword research should focus on business relevance and search intent, not just search volume.

3. Content should be mapped to specific stages of the customer journey.

4. On-page SEO fundamentals must be completed before advanced tactics are applied.

5. Internal linking helps distribute authority across important pages.

6. Backlink acquisition should focus on relevance and quality over quantity.

7. Performance tracking is necessary to identify what is working and what needs improvement.

Research Notes:

This video provides a structured framework for planning and executing SEO campaigns using a repeatable checklist approach.
